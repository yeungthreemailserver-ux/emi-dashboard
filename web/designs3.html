Option 3 — how to separate the big sections (pick A / B / C)

Same left-rail tree + chips. Only the section divider between layers differs.

3A — Strong grey rule + gap

A 2px slate rule and a little space above every layer. Clean, neutral, unambiguous breaks.

3B — Layer-colored top rule + header band

Each section starts with a thick rule in the layer's own color, plus a light band on the header row. Color-codes the sections.

A blank divider band between layers — separation by space rather than a heavy line. Most breathing room.
