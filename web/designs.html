EMI — choose a design (1–5)

Same sample data in 5 styles. Look at how each separates Layer › Sub-layer › Company. Reply with a number.

Option 1 — Institutional Clean (FactSet / Capital IQ)

White, no cell fills. Growth is green/red text. Layers = solid blue header band; sub-layers carry a colored accent edge; companies plain. Calmest, most serious-finance.

Option 2 — Refined Heat Grid

Keeps heat fills but with clean gridlines, soft pastels and bold layer bands — a polished conditional-format grid.

Option 3 — Left-rail Tree (Bloomberg-ish)

Colored left rail + tree connectors show the hierarchy; compact; growth as small chips (not full-cell fills).

Option 4 — Sectioned Cards

Each layer is its own card with whitespace between them — hierarchy by physical separation. Most breathing room.

Option 5 — Editorial Report (minimal)

Typography-led; growth as ▲/▼ + number; layers in uppercase with a heavy rule; tree-style indentation. Most "research report" feel.
